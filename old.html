Scalable, Maintainable, Simple Technology


PHP 5.4, 5.5, or 5.6

We support PHP 5.4 & up as it's nearly universally available. Shared hosting or a small VPS should be fine, as we've done everything we can to make our system as robust as possible. On average, each PHP process takes less than 1 Mb of memory, and generates each page in less than 15ms!

No SQL Database Required

To make things as easy as possible, we've crafted a custom flat-file database system that is incredibly robust. Things run smoothly for tens of thousands of incidents in your database, without you ever having to touch SQL.

Scalable Caching Everywhere

We've created a custom caching solution that works out of the box with no configuration. This caching solution works for your homepage, your history page, individual incident pages, the RSS feed, and the API. This way, you'll never have to worry about your status page slowing down.
